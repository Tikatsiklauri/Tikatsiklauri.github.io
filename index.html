ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Tika, a Software Developer based in New York City with a background in Fine Arts, concentration in modern dance. Although I've danced my whole life, I've always felt more fulfilled weaving between dance and academics like math and science classes, which allowed me to use both my creative, as well as analytical abilities, and coding has continued to stimulate both of these passions— creativity and problem-solving. I have experience in JavaScript, TypeScript, React/Redux, Ruby on Rails, and other full-stack technologies. Although I enjoy it all, my interests heavily land in frontend development. Something about working with React, writing code, and being able to see the resolution is so rewarding.

I'm looking for a collaborative, challenging environment where I can work with inspiring engineers, learn from them and grow as a Software Developer. I'd love to contribute to something meaningful, be part of a team, and solve problems.
